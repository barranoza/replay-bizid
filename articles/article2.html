The Ultimate Guide to a Balanced Diet

By Michael Chen, RD • March 25, 2024 • 6 Min Read

Eating a balanced diet is vital for good health and wellbeing. Food provides our bodies with the energy, protein, essential fats, vitamins, and minerals to live, grow, and function properly.

Understanding Macronutrients

To keep it simple, your meals should be built around the three main macronutrients: Proteins, Carbohydrates, and Fats. They each serve drastically different functions.

  • Proteins: Essential for building and repairing tissues. Found in meat, beans, and lentils.
  • Carbohydrates: Your body's primary energy source. Opt for complex carbs like oats and sweet potatoes.
  • Fats: Vital for hormone production and nutrient absorption. Avocados and olive oil are excellent sources.
Rule of Thumb: Fill half your plate with colorful vegetables, a quarter with lean protein, and a quarter with complex carbohydrates.

The Role of Superfoods

While no single food can cure everything, incorporating nutrient-dense foods like blueberries, spinach, and wild-caught salmon can give your immune system an incredible boost.
